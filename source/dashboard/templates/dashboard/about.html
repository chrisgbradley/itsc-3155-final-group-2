Welcome to EduWatch
EduWatch is a dashboard dedicated to providing students with data that may be relevant to them throughout their college career. Using data provided by the Office of Planning, Evaluation and Policy Development (OPEPD), EduWatch allows you to access statistics such as admission rates, graduations rates, and even net tuition cost per year. Browse the data to see where you might fall compared to other students!
