Welcome to EduWatch
EduWatch is a dashboard dedicated to providing students with data that may be relevant to them throughout their college career. Using data provided by the Office of Planning, Evaluation and Policy Development (OPEPD), EduWatch allows you to access statistics such as admission rates, graduations rates, and even net tuition cost per year. Browse the data to see where you might fall compared to other students!





Gathered Since Number of Colleges Tracked Estimated Total of Graduates Estimated Debt Total
1996 2981 36.81 Million 1.85 Trillion


The data you can access and browse through within the EduWatch dashboard is a compilation of data gathered from college students since 1996. Thanks to how long this data has been in the process of being collected, our data set has managed to compile information from across 2981 different colleges. Within this time, there has been a reported total of 36.81 million college graduates in this time. Our data set also provides information such as how much dept these college students have collected, which is currently estimated to be around 1.85 trillion dollars.
